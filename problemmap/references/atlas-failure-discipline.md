# Atlas failure discipline

## Source of truth
- Upstream repo: `WFGY/ProblemMap/Atlas/adapter-failure-discipline-v1.md`

## Rules
- Route first, then repair.
- Do not collapse primary and secondary into a vague summary.
- Do not inflate confidence when evidence is partial or weak.
- Do not use `no-fit` as a shortcut when the evidence simply needs more work.
- Do not turn a first repair move into a fake full repair plan.
- Do not let exemplars replace the structural routing grammar.

## Operational effect
If evidence is weak, keep the diagnosis unresolved and block experience writeback.
